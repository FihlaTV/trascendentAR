welcome to trascendentAR!

Here you will find the contents needed to learn the creation of augmented reality applications with trascendentAR

TrascendentAR uses libGDX game development framework, so it will be easy to pick up for people experienced with it. If you are new to the world of videogames and augmented reality applications, we suggest to start with "Configuring your project" and then "Simple App" tutorials where you will make an augmented reality app from zero. {% include side_menu.html %}
