About Transendent-AR license

TrascendentAR is licensed under LGPLv3 with a special modification taken from ARToolKit license

Full compliance requires reading the GNU General Public License: and following its directives, but some things that you must do include:

  • If your program is interactive, you need to display to users the fact that your program includes trascendentAR and ARToolKit under the LGPLv3 license, and provide either the full text of the GPLv3 and LGPLv3 license or a link to these. An "About" dialog is a good place to place this acknowledgement and notice. For iOS apps, a Settings pane is also a good place to show this notice.
  • If you modify the source of ARToolKit and then use this modified source in your program, you need to make available the source of your modifications of ARToolKit. The best way to do this is to fork the ARToolKit public repository on GitHub and publish your modifications in your fork.
NOTE: TrascendentAR is and integration between ARToolKitv5 (LGPLv3) and libGDX(Apache 2.0), therefore the only compatible license for trascendentAR is LGPLv3.
